How engagements run

A lightweight, documented path keeps everyone aligned from the first mapping session to the final handoff.

  1. Discover

    Clarity sessions to map the workflow, surface constraints, and lock the signals that prove success.

    • Async intake followed by a working session around jobs-to-be-done.
    • Inventory of systems, data access, and approval gates.

  2. Design

    Prototype agents and automations, stress-test data flows, and plan the governance story.

    • Clickable or running prototypes for fast feedback.
    • Architecture notes covering models, triggers, and human-in-the-loop safeties.

  3. Deliver

    Implement, document, train, and iterate with built-in feedback loops before the final handoff.

    • Enablement sessions and Loom walkthroughs for your team.
    • Runbooks, metrics snapshot, and backlog for the next release.
Weekly async status updates, Loom walkthroughs, and a shared workspace keep stakeholders in the loop without adding meeting overhead.
